About our Tablet

The Treasure Tablet (TT) is an all in one pill that treats and assists users with possible health concerns. The first step is the diagnosis, our pill emanates zinc, magnesium, iron, and poly(Lactic)-co-glycolic throughout the human body to receive the information on health concerns and transfer the data to our app. Next, TT reccomends healthy food options that our users need to follow in order to combat their health problems. We provide a step by step diet on they types of food you should take in and at what time is most beneficial to consume reccomended foods. Thirdly, we provide a fitness evaluator, which tracks what workouts we reccomend our users take, along with how often the workouts need to be done. Lastly, an indicator, which allows the user to view how severe their health concern is, we have three levels, green, orange, and red. Green, you have mild health concerns, orange means you have a more serious illness, and red means you're in dire need of assistance.
